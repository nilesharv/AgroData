AgroData (Know your Agriculture)


Provides agricultural statistics, as agriculture is one of the dominated field which affect economy of country like India, so many people are interested to know growth of agriculture and to support agriculture by making plans or by investing their money.

Who will get benefit?


1. Farmer
2. Bank
3. Government
4. Other investor.
5. People.
6. Student.
7. Agriculture based industry.

What will they get?


1. Comparison of earlier and latest data.
2. Cost comparison.
3. Production comparison.
4. Cultivation area information.
5. Cultivation information related to various crops.
6. Growth in crops.

All data will be in analytical form so that taking decision will be easy for viewer.
